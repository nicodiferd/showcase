Redefining Transportation in SLO

Bandit is creating a safe, reliable, and enjoyable transportation experience that enhances every night out and special event. We're committed to reducing drunk driving and promoting smarter choices in our community.

Our Mission

We're addressing the transportation gap that traditional options fail to fill, particularly for students and young adults when rideshare prices surge and wait times become unpredictable.

Services

Bandit provides transportation for nights out, special events, and more, turning getting around into an integral part of the experience rather than an afterthought.

Community

By partnering with local businesses, event organizers, and student groups, we're creating a transportation network that strengthens the San Luis Obispo community.
